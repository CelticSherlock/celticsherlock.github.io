About Fimmion Foods

Fimmion foods is a family-run restaurant, created in the year 433 in the third era. Our founder, Fimmion, was a prisoner trapped on a island. The only thing keeping him relatively sane was his love for food. When his captor, Lord Sheogorath, was released back into his original form of Jyggalag - the denizens of the Shivering isles were free to leave, before the new Lord Sheogorath could corrupt their minds further. After escaping, Fimmion created our company as an homage to what he loved the most, and our famous sweet rolls will bring you joy for years to come.
